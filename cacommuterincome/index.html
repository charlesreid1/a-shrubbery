CA Counties: Commute Means by Income

The following map gives a breakdown of how people get to work, based on the ratio of their income to the poverty line.

The population falls into three classes: (1) income below the poverty line; (2) income at or 1.5 times the poverty line; (3) income more than 1.5 times the poverty line.
